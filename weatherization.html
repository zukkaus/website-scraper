What Is the Weatherization Program?

This is a program funded and guided by the U.S. Department of Energy to assist low-income families in saving energy and reducing utility costs. Improvements are made at no cost to qualifying households.

Energy Saving Tips

  • Drapes — Where windows face the sun, keep drapes open during the day and close them at night.
  • Programmable Thermostat — Use one to automatically control the heat at night and when you are not at home.
  • Air Sealing — Seal gaps around doors and windows to prevent drafts.
  • Insulation — Proper attic and wall insulation significantly reduces heating and cooling costs.
