  • Community

    Serving Families Across the Region

    Washita Valley CAC provides a lifeline to hundreds of families each year — from early childhood education to housing stability and energy assistance. Our programs meet people where they are and help them build a better future.

    Learn about our mission →
  • Housing

    A Safe Home Changes Everything

    Stable housing is the foundation of a healthy life. Our housing program provides counseling, advocacy, and direct assistance to help qualifying families find and keep safe, affordable homes in our community.

    Explore housing services →
  • Weatherization

    Lower Bills, Warmer Homes

    Our weatherization technicians improve insulation, seal air leaks, and upgrade heating systems in homes across the region — reducing energy costs and improving comfort for income-qualifying households at no cost to them.

    Learn about weatherization →
