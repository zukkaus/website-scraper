Parent and community voice in the Head Start program.

About the Policy Council

The Head Start / Early Head Start Policy Council is a governance body made up of parents and community representatives. It plays a vital role in the planning, operation, and oversight of WVCAC Head Start programs.

Policy Council members have a say in decisions about program design, staff hiring, and budgeting — ensuring the program serves families the way families need it to.

    ✎ Staff Setup — Connect Your Google Sheet

    1. Open your Google Sheet with meeting dates.
    2. Make sure columns are in this exact order:
      Date  |  Time  |  Title  |  Description
      Date format: MM/DD/YYYY   e.g. 07/15/2025
    3. In Google Sheets: File → Share → Publish to web
    4. Choose Sheet1 and format CSV, then click Publish.
    5. Copy the URL Google gives you.
    6. Open build_pages.py and find this line:
      const SHEET_CSV_URL = ...
      Replace the placeholder with your URL, then run python3 build_pages.py.

    After setup: Just edit the Google Sheet — the calendar updates automatically. No code needed.
